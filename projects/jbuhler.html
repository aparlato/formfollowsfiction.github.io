jake stories

The story time I've designed is based on archetypes Joseph Campbell found in his dense research of myths. In his book, Hero With A Thousand Faces, Campbell explains that all myths follow the same narrative structure and character archetypes. The structure breaks down into three acts with four sections each. The first act is the call to adventure, the second is the challenges the hero has to face in the supernatural/unfamiliar world he is called to, and the third is the return to world she/he came from.
